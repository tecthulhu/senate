# LAW-010: Continuous Improvement

## Governance Gap Reporting

If any constituent project identifies a governance gap, missing rule, or needed policy enhancement, it must submit a bill to the senate (`kescott027/senate`) via the appropriate issue template.

## Required Content

Every bill addressing a governance gap must include:

- **Project context**: What type of project and what phase it is in.
- **Gap description**: What is missing or ambiguous in current governance.
- **Impact**: Risk, rework, or delivery/safety impact caused by the gap.
- **Proposed law change**: The specific text or policy change being proposed.
- **Evidence**: Links to the story, PR, incident, or blocker that surfaced the gap.

## Execution Requirement

The gap must be tracked as an active blocker or backlog item in the constituent project until the senate bill is filed. Filing the bill resolves the tracking obligation, but the underlying gap remains open until the senate acts on the bill.
